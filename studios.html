Studio

Maintaining quality of life outside of work fuels the creativity, curiosity, and energy we bring to our games. We believe in working with ridiculously talented people who love what they do, whether they’re working or playing. Salt Lake City is surrounded by the beautiful Wasatch mountains, world-class skiing and snowboarding, renowned National Parks, and sits in the center of a vibrant, diverse, global innovation technology hub with some of the greatest entrepreneurs and companies in the world, making it one of the most congenial cities in the U.S. to cultivate a rich work-life balance for our employees.
